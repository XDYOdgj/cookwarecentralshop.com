Experience the Future of Smart Cleaning with DEEBOT T30S

Meet the all-in-one robot vacuum designed to transform your cleaning routine. With advanced features like 11000Pa suction power and edge mopping technology, it's the perfect solution for a clean, healthy home.

Powerful Cleaning Performance

Equipped with 11000Pa suction power, the DEEBOT T30S delivers unmatched strength to lift dirt, dust, and debris from carpets and hard floors with ease. Whether you're tackling everyday messes or stubborn stains, this vacuum ensures a spotless finish every time.

Edge Mopping Technology

Thanks to TruEdge Adaptive Edge Mopping, the DEEBOT T30S can clean around furniture and moldings with 1mm precision. This technology ensures 99% coverage on hard-to-reach areas, leaving no corners untouched.

ZeroTangle Advantage

Designed for hassle-free maintenance, ZeroTangle technology prevents hair from getting tangled in the brush, making cleaning smoother and more efficient. Say goodbye to the frustration of tangled hair and hello to a cleaner home.

Smart and Intuitive Operation

Control your DEEBOT T30S with voice commands, a simple foot touch, or compatibility with Alexa and Apple Watch. This makes it the ideal robot vacuum for hands-free cleaning anywhere in your home.

All-in-One OMNI Station Mode

Enjoy the convenience of an all-in-one OMNI station with self-emptying, auto water refill, and hot-water mop washing. This innovative system keeps your vacuum powered and ready without constant manual intervention.

Smart Navigation and Safety

With smart navigation and obstacle avoidance, the DEEBOT T30S ensures efficient cleaning while avoiding damage to your furniture and flooring. It keeps your home fresh and clean without interruption.

Perfect for Every Home

The DEEBOT T30S is an all-in-one robot vacuum that combines smart cleaning mode, powerful suction, and advanced edge mopping technology. It's ideal for maintaining cleanliness on both carpets and hard floors, making your life easier and your space cleaner.
