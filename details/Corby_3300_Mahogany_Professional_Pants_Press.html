Introducing the Corby 3300 Pants Press - Timeless Precision for Modern Wardrobes

Since 1930, the Corby 3300 Pants Press has been trusted by professionals and home users alike. Known for its durable build and user-friendly design, this pressing machine is the ideal partner for those who value quality and efficiency. Whether you're looking to press suits or trousers, the Corby 3300 is built to deliver outstanding results with ease.

Why Choose the Corby 3300?

With a 30-minute cycle, the Corby 3300 ensures even pressing for all your lighter fabrics, including suits and trousers. Its patented stretcher bars are specifically designed to tackle stubborn wrinkles around the knees, giving you a smooth, wrinkle-free finish every time.

The mahogany finish is not only elegant but also functional, blending seamlessly with any room or workspace. Its compact size and versatility allow for wall or doormount installation, making it perfect for small spaces. Plus, it comes with a hanger and feet for easy setup and use.

Whether you're a busy professional needing quick access to a pressing solution or a fashion enthusiast looking to maintain a spotless wardrobe, the Corby 3300 is the perfect choice. It combines classic functionality with a sleek, stylish design that complements your home or office environment.
