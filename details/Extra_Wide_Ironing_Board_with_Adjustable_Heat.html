Effortless Ironing Made Simple

Introducing the Jakeefud Extra Wide Ironing Board, your perfect solution for smoother, faster, and more efficient ironing in any setting. Designed with both home users and busy professionals in mind, this ironing board combines practicality with style to make your daily routine easier.

With an impressive size of 13" x 43", this board provides extra room to iron larger garments and pants without worrying about bending or stretching. The adjustable height feature, offering 7 comfortable levels, ensures you can tailor your ironing experience to your perfect posture and comfort.

Featuring a steam mesh top, it allows for efficient steam treatment to lift stubborn wrinkles and smooth fabrics with ease. Its anti-slip feet provide stability when storing the board away, ensuring it doesn't wobble or slide during use. The padded cover offers reliable protection for your clothing, keeping it safe from scratches or damage while also adding durability to the board itself.

Key Features

  • Padded Cover
  • Extra Wide Size (13" x 43")
  • Height Adjustable (7 Levels)
  • Steam Mesh Top
  • Anti-Slip Feet

Whether you're ironing for work or personal use, the Jakeefud Ironing Board is built to last, offering a professional finish at home. Its combination of comfort, durability, and efficiency makes it a must-have for every laundry room or workspace.
