Powerful steam cleaning meets ergonomic design with the Conair Turbo ExtremeSteam Full Size Garment Steamer

Say goodbye to wrinkles and stains with this high-performance garment steamer. Built for both professionals and everyday users, it combines robust power with intuitive design to deliver a seamless steam cleaning experience.

Unmatched Steam Power

Equipped with 1875W steam power, the Turbo ExtremeSteam Full Size Garment Steamer delivers intense steam for deep cleaning and spot removal. Whether you're dealing with delicate fabrics or tough stains, you'll find this steamer up to the challenge.

Ergonomic Design for Easy Use

The compact and lightweight design makes it easy to handle and maneuver. The telescoping upright allows for ergonomic height adjustments, ensuring comfort and efficiency during every use.
