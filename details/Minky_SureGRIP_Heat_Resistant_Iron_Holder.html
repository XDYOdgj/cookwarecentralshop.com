Minky SureGRIP Heat Resistant Iron Holder

Keep your iron safe and secure with our Minky SureGRIP Heat Resistant Iron Holder. Designed with an adjustable safety strap, it ensures a firm grip and worry-free storage. The heat-resistant silicone and nylon hanger protect your iron from warping, even when hot, making it a reliable solution for any ironing routine. With its universal fit, it accommodates all standard steam irons and can be mounted to a wall or door for easy access and organization.

The compact 12 x 6.7 x 6.5-inch design of the SureGRIP Iron Holder fits neatly next to your ironing board, saving valuable space. Its included wall mount hardware makes it simple to store when not in use, helping to keep your workspace tidy. The seamless cable storage back of the caddy keeps your cords organized and out of sight, offering a hidden yet essential addition to your ironing essentials.
