Wall-Mount Ironing Board Hanger with 360-Degree Adjustable Slope

Transform your laundry room or home kitchen with this sleek, space-saving wall mount ironing board hanger. Designed to effortlessly attach to walls or over doors, it keeps your ironing essentials organized and out of the way.

Adjustable 360-Degree Slope

Whether you're working on a small board or a large one, this hanger provides a perfectly adjustable slope that ensures your ironing is efficient and comfortable. Its 360-degree angle allows optimal placement for any user preference.

Universal Compatibility & Heavy-Duty Build

Fits both T-shaped and Y-shaped leg boards without any issues. Built with heavy-duty stainless steel, it supports up to 35 lbs and is designed to last. The secure cover keeps your tools safe and prevents any accidental drops.
